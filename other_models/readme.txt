This folders contains 3 popular CPG models based on different graph topopogy
1. Fully coupled (fully_coupled1.py): 
Righetti, Ludovic, and Auke Jan Ijspeert. "Pattern generators with sensory feedback for the control of quadruped locomotion." 2008 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation. IEEE, 2008.

2. Nearest neighbor bidirectional coupling (salamander.py)
Ijspeert, Auke Jan, et al. "From swimming to walking with a salamander robot driven by a spinal cord model." science 315.5817 (2007): 1416-1420.

3. Diffusuve coupling (diffusive.py)
Yu, Haitao, et al. "Gait generation with smooth transition using CPG-based locomotion control for hexapod walking robot." IEEE Transactions on industrial electronics 63.9 (2016): 5488-5500.
